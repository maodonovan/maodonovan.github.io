3.3 By whom were you (or the person you know) physically assaulted?

Respondents who had been assaulted in court were asked who the assailant was. The way the question was phrased allowed for respondents to cite more than one assailant. 91 assailants were indicated. The single largest category (21) were SAPS officers, followed by ‘members of the public’ and ‘other’ with 16 counts each. Prosecutors were cited in six instances, and the accused in two instances. Correctional service officers, court security staff and interpreters were each cited once.
