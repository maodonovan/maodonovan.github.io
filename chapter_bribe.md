 Who solicited a bribe
--------------------

63 requests for bribes/ gratuities were asked of the 933 respondents. SAPS members accounted for almost half (43\%) of all 'requests". No 'requests' from magistrates or interpreters were recorded. There was notable variation in the pattern of who asked for bribes between the six courts. Two third of solicitations came from Cape Town and Gqebhera courts.

Almost half (40%) of bribe requests in Mthatha court were from lawyers. Presumably those soliciting bribes were legal aid lawyers who, unlike commercial lawyers, may not request payment for services. However there are very few observations from Mthatha. Oudtshoorn, Paarl and King Williams Town so some circumspection is required in interpretation.
